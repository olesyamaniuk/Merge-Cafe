About the Game

Step into Merge Cafe - Restaurant Decor, a delightful merging game where you help Cindy, a budding chef, create the most famous cafe in town! Combine ingredients, discover new recipes, and decorate your restaurant with stylish furniture. Every choice you make helps Cindy bring her dream cafe to life.
Merge ingredients to create exciting new dishes.
Decorate and upgrade your cafe to attract special customers.
Solve fun challenges and unlock new menu items.

How to Play

  • Merge Ingredients

    Combine items to discover new recipes and enhance your menu.

  • Decorate Your Cafe

    Choose furniture, wallpapers, and layouts to make your cafe unique.

  • Serve Customers

    Complete customer orders to earn rewards and unlock upgrades.

  • Solve Challenges

    Take on special quests and events to gain exclusive rewards.

  • Expand Your Business

    Upgrade Cindy’s cafe and make it the best restaurant in town!
